BOOK OF WISDOM

A collection of sayings and reflections
✦ ✧ ✦
Chapter I
1. Seek understanding before judgment, and knowledge before speech.
2. The patient heart gathers what haste leaves behind.
3. Let truth be your companion, and humility your guide.
4. A lamp may light a room, yet wisdom illuminates a lifetime.
❦
Chapter II
1. The river reaches the sea not by force, but by persistence.
2. Honor knowledge wherever it is found, and goodness wherever it appears.
